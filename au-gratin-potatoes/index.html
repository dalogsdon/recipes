Au Gratin Potatoes

adapted from

Ingredients

  1. gold potatoes
  2. 3 tbsp butter
  3. 3 tbsp flour
  4. 1 tsp dry mustard
  5. 1/2 tsp onion powder
  6. 2 cups milk
  7. 1 cup extra sharp cheddar

Steps

  1. Oven: 400 F
  2. Mandoline slice potatoes 1/8 inch thick
  3. Arrange potatoes vertically in baking dish
  4. Melt butter on low heat
  5. Add flour to butter
  6. Whisk spices into butter
  7. Whisk milk into butter
  8. Whisk milk on med/high until thick
  9. Turn off heat
  10. Whisk cheese into milk
  11. Pour cheese sauce over potatoes
  12. Cover potatoes with foil
  13. Bake for 1 hour
  14. Uncover potatoes
  15. Bake for 30 min
